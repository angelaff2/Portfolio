Roby, Galactic Wolf Girl

In a graphic design class, I created a character illustration using GIMP, a graphics editing and creation program. This character was the basis for a larger project where I worked with Blender. Through this process, I learned how to combine graphic design and 3D modeling tools to bring my ideas to life.
